El camino

El Camino is a 2019 crime-drama film written and directed by Vince Gilligan, the creator of Breaking Bad. It is a sequel to the award-winning series that follows former meth kingpin Jesse Pinkman (Aaron Paul) as he embarks on an epic journey of redemption after narrowly escaping the events of the series finale. Along the way, Jesse encounters allies and adversaries while dealing with old traumas and fearing his captors' return. Through risky decisions and heart wrenching moments, El Camino tells a story of reunion, despair, hope and healing.
